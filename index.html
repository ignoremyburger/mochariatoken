What is Mocharia Token?

Mocharia Token is a simple ERC-20 token hosted on Binance Smart Chain with the purpose of simplifying payments. In the near future, this token will be used for our coffee shop (plans will be updated in the subreddit). Until then, get yourself some $CHAR tokens!

Limited supply

Only 1 million $CHAR will ever be issued, there are no mint functions. The price can't fluctuate and the developers can't print money

Community owned

We are in the process of locking 100% of our tokens into PancakeSwap with multiple Liquidity Providers ensuring a stable exchange rate and become truly community owned.
